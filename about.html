    • Our Goals

    The goal of this website is to bring awareness to our everyday behaviours and how they affect the environment, as well as quantitative and lifestyle suggestions on how to reduce our impacts.

    Our product addresses 3 UN Sustainable Development Goals:

    Goal 13: Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts
    Goal 14: Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources
    Goal 15: Sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, halt and reverse land degradation, halt biodiversity loss

    The target audience of our product is the 59% of the global population with internet access. Whether rich or poor, there is a consistent lack of education on reducing personal environmental impact. Through our product we allow users to realize their environmental impacts and their consequences, as well as promote sustainable behavior such as walking/biking instead of driving, reducing needless consumerism, retiring plastic habits, and eating more sustainable food.
