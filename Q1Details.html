Goal 12: Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns: Fossil fuels are non-renewable resources that are derived from crude oil within the Earth. Driving more directly leads to an increased consumption in crude oil products, requiring more to be extracted from the Earth. Resultantly, the limited supply of this resource decreases. In addition, methods of extraction are usually damaging to local ecosystems. For example, the oil tar sands in Alberta require massive local excavation, disrupting native biodiversity.
