    What Is Our Goal?


    As a result of increased global warming and climate change, us humans and the Earth we live on are facing gradual changes that could lead to natural catastrophies and calamities if we continue our unsustainable production, consumption, and pollution. In order to protect our planet and all the organisms that live on it, the United Nations declared specific goals that we all should work towards solving. These are known as the United Nations Sustainable Development GoalsThe goal of Enviro-Score is to educate the public so we can all contribute towards these goals.

    Goal 13: Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts

    Goal 14: Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources

    Goal 15: Sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, halt and reverse land degradation, halt biodiversity loss
